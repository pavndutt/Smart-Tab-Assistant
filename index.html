Smart Tab Assistant

Your all-in-one assistant to auto-group inactive tabs, close duplicates, track active tab time, and save links with custom notes.

Add to Chrome (It's Free)

Top Features

  • Auto-Sleep Inactive Tabs: Frees your memory by grouping tabs you haven't touched recently.
  • Duplicate Cleaner: Close 10 identical sites in one click.
  • Active Time Tracking: See exactly where your time goes, complete with privacy-first graphics.
  • Save with Notes: Declutter your window by saving tabs with custom annotations for later.
